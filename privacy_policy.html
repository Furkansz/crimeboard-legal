Crimeboard Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-07

Crimeboard does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to the developer or to third parties.

Data collection

Crimeboard does not require account creation and does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

Local storage

Gameplay progress, language selection, sound settings, and similar preferences are stored locally on the user’s device so the game can preserve state between sessions. This information is not transmitted to external servers.

Permissions and sensitive data

Crimeboard does not collect precise location, contacts, photos, camera input, microphone input, payment information, health data, messages, or web browsing data.

Children

Crimeboard is not directed primarily to children under 13.

Changes

If Crimeboard’s data practices change in a future version, this privacy policy will be updated before that version is released.
